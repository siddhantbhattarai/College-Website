Undergraduate & Postgraduate Programs

ISMT British College offers a range of internationally recognized programs in collaboration with the University of Sunderland and Pearson BTEC. Our courses are designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills needed for successful careers in today's competitive global market.

Information Technology Programs

BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering

A comprehensive program that covers computer hardware, software development, networking, and systems integration. Graduates are prepared for careers in IT infrastructure management, software development, and systems engineering.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Awarding Body: University of Sunderland, UK
Know More

BSc (Hons) Cyber Security and Digital Forensics

This program focuses on network security, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and cybercrime investigation. Students develop skills to protect organizations from cyber threats and investigate security breaches.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Awarding Body: University of Sunderland, UK
Know More

Business Programs

BA (Hons) Business and Management (BBA)

A comprehensive business program covering marketing, finance, human resources, operations, and strategic management. Graduates are prepared for careers in various business sectors or to start their own ventures.

BSc (Hons) International Tourism and Hospitality Management

Postgraduate Programs

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Our MBA program is designed for professionals seeking to enhance their management skills and advance their careers. The curriculum covers advanced business concepts, leadership, strategic management, and global business practices.

  • Duration: 1.5 years
  • Awarding Body: University of Sunderland, UK
Know More
BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering
Program Overview

This program provides a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, software development, networking, and systems integration. Students learn to design, implement, and manage IT infrastructure and software solutions.

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Awarding Body: University of Sunderland, UK
  • Entry Requirements: Higher Secondary with minimum 60% in Science/Math
  • Intake: September & February
Key Modules
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Computer Architecture
  • Database Systems
  • Web Development
  • Networking Essentials
  • Software Engineering
  • System Analysis and Design
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Cloud Computing
  • Final Year Project
Career Prospects

Graduates can pursue careers as:

  • Software Developer
  • Systems Engineer
  • Network Administrator
  • IT Project Manager
  • Database Administrator
