Our Story

Established in 2011, ISMT British College has been at the forefront of providing internationally recognized qualifications in Nepal. Our institution was founded with the vision of bridging the gap between Nepalese education and global standards.

Through our strategic collaborations with the University of Sunderland and Pearson BTEC, we offer programs that are recognized worldwide, giving our students the opportunity to receive a quality education without leaving their home country.

Over the years, we have grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in Nepal, with a strong focus on academic excellence, practical learning, and career development.

Our Mission

To deliver industry-relevant education that prepares students for global careers by providing quality teaching, modern facilities, and practical learning experiences.

We strive to create an educational environment that fosters critical thinking, innovation, and personal growth, enabling our students to become leaders in their chosen fields.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier institution for international education in Nepal, known for academic excellence, innovation, and producing graduates who make significant contributions to society.

We aim to expand our program offerings, enhance our facilities, and strengthen our international partnerships to provide even more opportunities for our students.
