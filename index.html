Excellence in Education

ISMT British College is a premier educational institution established in 2011, offering internationally recognized qualifications in collaboration with the University of Sunderland and Pearson BTEC.

Our mission is to deliver industry-relevant education in Nepal, preparing students for global careers through quality teaching, modern facilities, and practical learning experiences.
