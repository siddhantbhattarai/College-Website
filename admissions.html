Admissions Process

ISMT British College welcomes applications from students who are passionate about learning and ready to embark on an international educational journey. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and supportive.

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the application form along with required documents including academic transcripts, identification proof, and passport-sized photographs.

2

Entrance Exam

Depending on the program, applicants may need to take an entrance exam to assess their aptitude and readiness for the chosen course.

3

Interview & Document Verification

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview. Original documents will be verified during this stage.

4

Offer Letter & Enrollment

Successful applicants will receive an offer letter. Upon acceptance and payment of the initial fees, students will be officially enrolled in their chosen program.

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completion of Higher Secondary Education (10+2) or equivalent
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks in Science/Commerce/Management stream
  • Proficiency in English language (IELTS 6.0 or equivalent for international students)
  • Successful completion of the ISMT entrance exam and interview
Required Documents
  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Copy of citizenship/passport
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • English language proficiency certificate (if applicable)

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Relevant work experience (preferred but not mandatory)
  • Proficiency in English language (IELTS 6.5 or equivalent for international students)
  • Successful completion of the ISMT entrance exam and interview
Required Documents
  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates (Bachelor's degree)
  • Copy of citizenship/passport
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • CV/Resume
  • Work experience letters (if applicable)
  • English language proficiency certificate (if applicable)
